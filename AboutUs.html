About Us

Our History...

Skye High originally started out as a summer learning program during 2020; but with the 3 founders and their dedication, innovation, aspiration for learning, has led this institution to where it is now. Voluntary funding turned into large-scale funding to put up an exceptional centre for education. Skye High's emphasis on not only the scientific branches but also the artistic branches of schooling is what made it stand out from the rest. In a short span of time, such a dream came true. Officially recognized and established as a science highschool on the 23rd of June on the 23rd year of the 2000s, Skye High has continued to uplift its reputation for being one of the best learning institutions; from its well-equipped faculties to its outstanding teachers and last but not least, its exceptional and determined students.
With our core values of yearning for success and knowledge, embracing the ideology of being yes-minded, and for our students to have incredible expertise, we ought to lead our students to greatness; that is, to exceed beyond their limits and achieve the impossible.
